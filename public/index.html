VIZ-A-THON
      A VIZ-A-THON is a two stage hackathon where students from colleges all over Pune can participate. In this event, students will be provided with a dataset and they have to create attractive and interesting visualizations using the provided dataset. Students are free to use any tools for the same purpose (eg. Tableau).
      Based on their performance the best teams performing will be shortlisted to give a presentation in front of our judges. The top 3 teams (as selected by our judges) will be eligible for cash prizes and all other teams will get exciting consolation prizes.
